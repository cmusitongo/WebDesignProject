Who Am I?

I'm a YouTuber! Just teaching what I've learned to others. Spreading some positivity to the world. Believe in yourself you'd be surprised what you discover.

I’m a Christian, a mother and a wife. I’m also in the process of completing my Law degree in the hope of sitting my bar exam in a few years and practicing law as a qualified solicitor. I love anything and everything beauty and hair related! Every single hair style is done by myself on my own head. All that I've learned is self taught. I started doing hair on myself at thirteen years old, along with my first clients. I have progressed in so many ways by constantly practice my craft and working hard at it.
