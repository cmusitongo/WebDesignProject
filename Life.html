Life Advice

The life advice I would give to others and especially the younger generation would be don’t give up on yourself! Always believe in yourself and put your best foot forward. Do not compare your progress or success to anyone else’s. We all have a different destiny! What is yours will not pass you by.

Below is a fun test that you can take to see how your outlook on life is. Feel free to take the quiz. Remember that it's only a guideline and don't let it get you down if you get a low score! Strive to prove the quiz wrong and do better for yourself! (NOTE: Make sure you read the intructions!)

Always Remember:

  • Always try remember the best is yet to come. Hardships too shall pass.
  • Doubt kills more dreams than failure ever will.
  • Never let go of your dreams, they might be closer than it seems! When it rains, look up to the sky and look for rainbows.
  • When it’s dark, look up towards the stars. Common sense is a flower which doesn’t grow in everyone’s garden, so don’t expect much.
  • A lot of problems in the world would disappear if we talked to each other instead of about each other.
  • At some point you just have to let go of what you thought should happen and live in what is happening.
