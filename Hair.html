Tips & Tutorials

Doing hair requires patience and determination. If you have the love or eagerness to learn you will be fine. It is something that you must continue to do. Practice makes perfect, you don’t give. To do hair, you must constantly work at it to get good at it. It doesn’t happen overnight!

I started by practicing on my dolls and from the age of 13 and continued to do it on my own hair. Shortly after that, I started receiving paying clients. Hair Braiding

Here are a few tips on braiding:

  • Make sure to section out your hair with either freehand or with a tail comb.
  • To start your plat, you can split your hair in three pieces or try and see what suits you best.
  • Take your time, practice makes perfect!
  • If you’re platting the hair and your hair is bumpy and not even, keep going!
  • Make sure not to pull it too tight! Make sure to straighten it with your finger
